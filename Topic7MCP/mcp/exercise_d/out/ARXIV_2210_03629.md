# Citation Neighborhood Report

## Summary
The seed paper titled **"ReAct: Synergizing Reasoning and Acting in Language Models"** (2022) explores the integration of reasoning and acting capabilities within large language models (LLMs). This approach, named ReAct, facilitates the generation of reasoning traces and task-specific actions in a coherent, interleaved manner. By allowing reasoning to inform action planning, ReAct enhances the model's performance across diverse decision-making tasks, yielding improved interpretability and effectiveness compared to existing methods. The empirical results showcase significant advancements in question answering and interactive decision-making benchmarks, demonstrating ReAct's superiority over traditional approaches that treat reasoning and acting as discrete processes.
